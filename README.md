Recommended Structure and Order
# 1. Project Title / Headline

📊 Sales Analytics Dashboard: Business Performance & Customer Insights

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, shipment efficiency, and payment trends. The report helps businesses monitor operational performance and uncover actionable insights through dynamic visual analytics.

---

# 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Sales Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI report developed to help businesses track and evaluate sales activities across multiple dimensions, including products, customers, shipments, and payments. The dashboard enables users to identify revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, product profitability, and operational performance through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

This project is intended for business analysts, sales managers, e-commerce teams, and decision-makers who require data-driven insights to improve sales strategies and operational efficiency.

---

# 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Main platform used to design and develop the interactive dashboard.
• 📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing.
• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Applied for KPI calculations, measures, conditional logic, and dynamic analysis.
• 🗂️ Data Modeling – Relationships between sales, customer, shipment, payment, and product datasets to enable cross-filtering and advanced analytics.
• 📈 Data Visualization – Interactive charts, KPI cards, slicers, tables, and trend analysis visuals.
• 📁 File Format – .pbix for dashboard development and .png for dashboard previews.

---

# 4. Data Source

Source: Sales and Business Transaction Dataset.

The dataset contains transactional sales data including:

• Customer information
• Product details
• Shipment categories and delivery data
• Payment methods and transaction status
• Revenue, profit, ratings, and order performance metrics
• Monthly and yearly sales trends

The dataset is structured into multiple related tables to support detailed business performance analysis and cross-functional reporting.

---

# 5. Features / Highlights

## • Business Problem

Businesses often struggle to monitor sales performance across different operational areas simultaneously. Raw transactional data makes it difficult to quickly answer important business questions such as:

• Which products generate the highest profit?
• How do customer purchasing behaviors change over time?
• Which shipment methods are most commonly used?
• What payment methods are preferred by customers?
• How do ratings and cancellations affect overall business performance?

Without an integrated analytics system, decision-making becomes slower and less efficient.

---

## • Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard was developed to:

• Monitor overall sales and operational performance
• Analyze customer behavior and purchasing patterns
• Evaluate product profitability and cancellation rates
• Track shipment and payment trends
• Support data-driven business decision-making through interactive analytics
